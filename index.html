About Us

Ethiopian Coffee is a prized treasure, celebrated for being the birthplace of coffee and renowned for its exceptional flavor and complex profile. Known for its floral aroma, and vibrant fruity notes, Ethiopian coffee is as diverse as the regions it comes from.

Read more

From the well-known Sidamo and Yirgacheffe to the more elusive Harrar and Limu, Ethiopian coffee offers an array of flavors, making it a favorite among coffee connoisseurs worldwide. Coffee in Ethiopia isn't just a crop-it's deeply woven into the cultural fabric of the nation, where coffee ceremonies are a significant part of daily life, and farmers take immense pride in their centuries-old traditions.

Cultivating Ethiopian coffee is no small feat. Farmers work in diverse and challenging conditions, from the remote, high-altitude regions to the more rugged, lowland areas. The journey from farm to cup is often long, involving numerous smallholder farmers who tend to their coffee trees with great care and attention. In many areas, farmers rely on traditional methods, passed down through generations, ensuring that the coffee produced is as unique as the landscape it grows in. Despite facing challenges such as limited infrastructure, fluctuating weather patterns, and unpredictable market conditions, Ethiopian farmers are committed to producing the highest quality coffee, which remains a symbol of national pride and identity.

At Hello Addis coffee Ltd, we believe that every small effort can lead to meaningful change, and we are committed to supporting the sustainable growth of the coffee industry. Through direct trade with farmers and partnerships with local cooperatives, we ensure that our customers enjoy high-quality Ethiopian coffee that benefits the hardworking farmers and their communities. We are proud to work with organizations like the Ethiopian Coffee Farmers Cooperative Union (ECFCU), which seeks to improve the livelihoods of coffee farmers by advocating for fair trade practices, better quality production, and a more sustainable future for the Ethiopian coffee industry.

Ethiopian coffee has been cultivated for centuries, and today, it continues to thrive thanks to the passion, dedication, and resilience of its farmers. At Hello Addis coffee Co. Ltd, we offer 100% premium-quality Ethiopian coffee for coffee lovers around the world, while supporting the hardworking farmers who make it all possible.
